Meng Ding

I am currently a Ph.D. student advised by Prof. Jinhui Xu and Prof. Kaiyi Ji at the State University of New York at Buffalo(UB). I am also very fortunate to closely work with Prof. Di Wang. Previously, I obtained my Bachelor's degree in Mathematics from China Agricultural University.

I am interested in broad areas of data privacy and data memorization. My recent work focuses on privacy-preserving optimization/statistics and understanding model behaviors in (private) deep learning.
