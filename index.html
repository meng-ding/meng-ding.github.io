About Me

I am currently a Ph.D. student advised by Prof. Jinhui Xu and Prof. Kaiyi Ji at the State University of New York at Buffalo (UB). I am also very fortunate to closely work with Prof. Di Wang.

Previously, I received my Bachelor’s degree in Mathematics from China Agricultural University and a second major in Economics from Peking University.

My research interest lies in using mathematical insights into deep learning to enable better and safer AI.
